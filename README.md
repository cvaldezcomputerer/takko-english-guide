# TakkoTaco

TakkoTaco is an unofficial English website for Takko Machi, a small garlic farming town in Aomori, Japan.

I built it while living and teaching English there. The town has almost no English presence online, so I wanted a place for my students to practice, and a real English guide to the town for anyone curious about it.

## What's on it

**English Learning** - Games and classroom tools built for Takko students and their teachers. Vocabulary games, letter writing practice, a multiplayer drawing game, classroom timers, quiz creators, and more.

**Town Guide** - An unofficial English guide to Takko Machi. Covers local shrines, the Takko-ben dialect, a daily dialect word, and live local weather. More sections are in progress.

## Goals

- Give students a fun way to practice English outside of class
- Make Takko Machi more findable and readable for English speakers
- Keep building out the town guide over time
